Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Numyn (“Numyn”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information in connection with:

  • The Numyn marketing website (including numyn.ca and any subdomains).
  • Our waitlist and early-access sign-up forms.
  • Emails and support requests you send to us.
  • Licensing, purchase, and account-related information.

Numyn is developed and operated by a sole proprietor based in Ontario, Canada. We aim to handle your personal information in line with applicable Canadian privacy requirements, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and any other provincial laws that may apply.

1. Scope and key concepts

This policy applies to personal information that we collect and control, such as information you provide through our website, waitlist forms, licensing system, or support channels. “Personal information” means information about an identifiable individual, such as a name, email address, or contact details.

Local bookkeeping data inside the Numyn desktop app is treated differently:

  • Numyn is designed as a primarily local desktop application. Your bookkeeping data (e.g., income, expenses, clients, suppliers) is stored on your own device by default.
  • We do not automatically receive or host your bookkeeping data on our own servers.
  • If cloud backup or sync features are introduced in the future, they will be clearly described, be optional, and this policy will be updated to explain how any additional data is handled.

2. What information we collect

2.1 Website, usage, and technical information

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, operating system, pages viewed, timestamps).
  • Basic usage information in an aggregated or de-identified form to understand how often the site is visited and which sections are most useful.
  • If we enable analytics in the future, non-identifying technical information such as approximate region, device type, and browsing behaviour on our site.

2.2 Waitlist and early-access sign-ups

If you join the Numyn waitlist or request early access, we may collect:

  • Your email address (required).
  • Your name and/or business name (optional).
  • Information about your business type or location, if you choose to share it (optional).

  • The content of your message and any attachments you choose to send.

3. How we use your information

We use personal information for purposes such as:

  • Providing access to Numyn and managing licensing and activation.
  • Sending waitlist updates, launch announcements, and product information (with your consent).

For marketing-type emails (like waitlist updates and launch news), we rely on your express consent. You can withdraw that consent at any time by using an unsubscribe option provided in the email (where available) or through the unsubscribe form on our waitlist page, or by contacting us directly.
