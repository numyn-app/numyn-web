Privacy Policy (Draft)

Last updated: [Set your date here]

This privacy policy is a draft starting point for Numyn. It does not constitute legal advice. You should review and customize this page, and consider getting professional legal review before launch.

1. Who we are

Numyn is bookkeeping software developed and operated by a sole proprietor based in Canada (currently operating under the business name K-Games & Collectables). For the purposes of this policy, “Numyn”, “we”, and “us” refer to the developer and operator of the Numyn software and related websites.

2. What this policy covers

This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in relation to:

  • The Numyn marketing website (e.g., numyn.ca and related subdomains).
  • Support and contact emails you send to addresses like support@numyn.ca.
  • Optional analytics or crash reporting services, if and when they are enabled.

This policy does not cover your own bookkeeping data that lives locally inside the Numyn desktop app, which is designed to store data on your own machine rather than in our cloud infrastructure.

3. Information we collect

3.1 Website and contact

We may collect:

  • Basic web server logs (IP address, browser type, approximate region, timestamps).
  • Information you choose to send via email (e.g., name, email address, business details, questions).
  • Information you submit through any future contact or feedback forms on the site.

3.2 Purchases and licensing

When you purchase a Numyn license, payments will be handled by a third-party provider (such as Lemon Squeezy). They may collect personal and payment information as part of the checkout process. We do not store your full payment card details on our own servers.

We may receive limited information from the payment provider, such as your name, email address, country, and license details, in order to manage your license and provide support.

3.3 Application data

Numyn is designed to function primarily as a local desktop application. By default, your bookkeeping records and financial data are stored on your own device. We do not automatically receive or store your local bookkeeping data on our servers.

If we introduce optional cloud features or backups in the future, those features will be clearly described and this policy will be updated to explain how any associated data is handled.

4. How we use information

  • To respond to questions, support requests, and feedback.
  • To provide license information, receipts, and important notices about Numyn.
  • To maintain and improve the website and overall user experience.
  • To understand general usage trends in an aggregated, non-identifying way.

5. Email and communications

If you contact us at support@numyn.ca or other official addresses, we will use the information you provide to respond to your inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and provide product-related updates where appropriate.

6. Third-party services

Numyn may use third-party providers for services such as:

  • Payment processing and licensing (e.g., Lemon Squeezy).
  • Website hosting (e.g., GitHub Pages).
  • Basic site analytics, if enabled.

These providers have their own privacy policies, which govern how they handle your information.

7. Data retention

We retain email communications and licensing records for as long as reasonably necessary to provide support, comply with legal requirements, and maintain business records. You can request that we delete certain information, subject to any legal obligations we may have to retain it.

8. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights related to your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. If you have questions or want to make a request, contact us at support@numyn.ca.

9. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above and may provide additional notice as appropriate.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how Numyn handles information, you can contact:

Email: support@numyn.ca

This page is a non-binding draft provided for planning purposes and should be reviewed and customized before launch. It does not replace professional legal advice.
